security practices

We treat your data as if it was our own

Your data security is a top priority at MetriMeo. Every day we thrive to ensure that our security is parallel with industry standards and compliance. Below is a breakdown of our security practices:

Data Access

We maintain a strict need to know basis for access to your data. Also, access to your credit score and/or report are only provided after your approval.

Data Transmission

We use the standard 128-bit encryption to protect the transmission of your data on our site.

Customer Payment Information

MetriMeo uses secured external third party payment processing services and does not process, store or transmit any payment card data.

Physical Security

MetriMeo leverages Amazon AWS for its data centers. AWS has several years of experience in designing. constructing and operating state of the art data centers. Also the production network is logically isolated from other networks such as QA, and Development.

Coding and Testing Practices

We leverage industry standard programming techniques such as having a documented development and quality assurance processes to ensure that we meet security standards.

Employee Access

We follow the principle of least privilege in how we grant access to your data to our employees to use in the diagnosing and resolving of incidents as well as to responding to customer support requests.
