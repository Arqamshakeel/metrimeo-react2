Do You Need a Privacy Policy?


Based on international laws and modern business practices, you need a privacy policy if you collect any personal information or personal data from users.

Personal data includes:

  • Names
  • Email Addresses
  • Credit Card Numbers
  • Location Data
  • Browsing Preferences
  • Device Information

These are only a few examples of commonly collected personal information. If you use marketing services, analytics software, payment portals, or emailing lists, you need a privacy policy.
